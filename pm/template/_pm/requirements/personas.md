# Personas

_The stakeholders who matter. Who they are, what they care about, what "done" means to them. Distilled from conversations — not invented._

> One persona per H2 section. Keep them tight — 3–5 bullets each, not a novel.

---

## Example shape

### Sandy — Operations Manager

**Background:** runs the clothing manufacturing business. 20 years in the industry. Comfortable with FileMaker; doesn't write code.

**What she cares about:**
- Reducing time her team spends on manual searches.
- Customers being able to find products without calling the office.
- Not breaking what already works.

**What "done" looks like for her:**
- Searches return results in under 5 seconds with images.
- She can demo it to her partner without prep.
- The existing FileMaker workflows are untouched.

**Watch-outs:**
- Color and branding matter to her — she'll notice "almost right."
- She defers technical decisions but reverses them later if she doesn't feel consulted.
